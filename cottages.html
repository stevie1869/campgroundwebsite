Cottages:at Ayers Lake

For more seclusion, try our Cottages

If you'd like even more seclusion, a stay at one of our rustic
cottages with modern facilities would be ideal. They're widely spaced
in a beautiful lakeside pine grove, away from the campground.
Plain and simple, but comfortable, the cottages feature three bedrooms, living-dining room, bath with shower, full kitchen,
and screened porch, picnic tables and charcoal grill.
Cottages are completely furnished although everything might not match. You bring your linens. Occupancy is limited to six persons. Due to environmental concerns, limit of 2 cars at each cottage.
Alternative parking may be arranged.

Cottage Rates

The cottages are currently undergoing long overdue repairs and updates. We will reopen next summer for seasonal and monthly rentals only. Please call for availability.
Cottages cannot be sublet.
Check in time is 4 p.m. and Check out time is 9 a.m.
