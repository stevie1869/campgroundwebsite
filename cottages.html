Cottages:at Ayers Lake

For more seclusion, try our Cottages

We have installed a gate at the entrance to the cottages for increased security and privacy. Please come to the farmhouse office to check in and get your key card for access.

If you'd like even more seclusion, a stay at one of our rustic
cottages with modern facilities would be ideal. They're widely spaced
in a beautiful lakeside pine grove, away from the campground.
Plain and simple, but comfortable, the cottages feature three bedrooms, living-dining room, bath with shower, full kitchen,
and screened porch, picnic tables and charcoal grill.
Cottages are completely furnished although everything might not match. You bring your linens; queen and twin beds. Each cottage now has its own internet connection, so you can work while at the lake. Coin operated laundry facilities available in the cottage grove. Occupancy is limited to six persons. Due to environmental concerns, limit of 2 cars at each cottage.
Alternative parking may be arranged.
