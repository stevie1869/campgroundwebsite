For Family Camping:

Ayers Lake Farm Campground is the place for quiet, rustic family camping:
No bright lights, no noisy rec hall, no entertainment.

Our Campground office is a big, old New England farmhouse.
The camping area is 15 secluded acres of forests, fields and shorefront.

You'll share our sunny beach area, the cool, clean water and fine views of Ayers Lake and
a country style welcome. Be sure to join us on your next vacation!

Please make your reservations now! Personal checks, money orders, credit cards, and cash accepted.
We are always busy during vacation season.
