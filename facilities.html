Facilities at Ayers Lake

Enjoy :

50 Rustic Sites :

  • Waterfront and pine grove location, all with fine views of the lake.

  • Fireplaces ( Wood and Ice available ), picnic tables.

  • Excellent boating, canoeing, sailing, and kayaking.

  • Fresh water swimming.

  • Picturesque New Hampshire Stone Walls.

  • Excellent bass fishing. ( Fishing licenses are available online for those 16 or older at www.nhfishandgame.com).

Convenient Facilities :

  • Electric, water, and septic hookups.

  • Dumping station for registered guests only.

  • Playground for very small children.

  • Hiking and Biking on country roads and forest trails.

  • Washer and dryer available. About $2.00 per load per machine.

  • Soda Machine, Wood and Ice available.

  • Fishing Dock (license required for anyone 16 and up.)
